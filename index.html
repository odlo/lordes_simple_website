Stage name Lorde, born Ella Marija Yelich-O'Connor, wrote Pure Heroine, all of its songs, at the age of 16. She wrote a generations defining album at 16. And then wrote Melodrama. Perfection. She was born in New Zealand, and takes long breaks between albums. As we wait for the release of her third album, we mourn the loss of her dog, Pearl.
